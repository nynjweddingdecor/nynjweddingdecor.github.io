About Us

As you begin your wedding journey, we, at NyNj Wedding Decor, ensure that we transform the vision of your dream wedding into a spectacular celebration. We tailor all wedding decor specially for each client – extravagant or intimate, modern or traditional, you think of it, and we make it happen!

We leave no stone unturned, from conceptualizing to designing décor in order to curate a remarkable wedding experience. We believe that wedding decor is a portrait of the bride and groom, and we pride ourselves in portraying it with excellence.
